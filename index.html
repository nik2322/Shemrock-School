SHEMROCK : The Success Story

One of the first playschool chains in India, SHEMROCK is recognized for having pioneered a dynamic and kid-friendly learning approach that has revolutionized the idea of early childhood education since 1989. With more than 550 branches providing the groundwork for success to over 400,000 children, SHEMROCK, the Award Winning School Chain, is a force to be reckoned with in the early education industry.
